Welcome to First Launceston Scouts

Part of one of the biggest youth shaped organisations in the world

Having first registered in March 1958, our group has gone from strength to strength and has seen many changes.

Our group welcomes all genders and backgrounds from 6 to 60+. Our sections run a varied program of activities both indoors and outdoors: you name it, we endeavour to do it! (see individual pages for more information).

We believe all young people thrive when learning through adventure and fun. We aim to provide this in a safe environment, allowing every person the chance to excel in their own way. All our adult volunteers give their time freely and undergo ongoing training to ensure your young person has a fun and adventurous time whilst being as safe as possible.
